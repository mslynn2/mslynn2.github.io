Attack of the Killer Iguanas

This is a map of hypothetical iguana landing zones in and around the city of Melbourne, Australia. The circles represent containment zones whereas each has a unique radius depending on that specific iguanas reach. Furthermore, the user is able to click throughout the map to distinguish travel routes that are deemed safe.

Hydro, Nuclear, and Wind Power Plants of the US

Comparison of power generation by Hydro, Nuclear, and Wind plants, respectively. Upon clicking once, the map will display power plants within 500 km of the click event. When double clicked, 200km from the click event.
